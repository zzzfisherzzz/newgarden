Our story

Precision design and meticulous attention to detail is at the bedrock of everything we do from compact garden offices to the large scale eco homes and commercial business premises. We design and manufacture buildings for nationwide installation but we keep our roots firmly grounded in the heart of Yorkshire where we can draw eco inspiration from the rolling Pennine hills, not to mention the historic and artistic Dean Clough Mills where our design studio is based. Our state of the art manufacturing facility is based nearby so our designers and project managers can maintain regular contact with our installation teams to ensure that your project runs smoothly from start to finish.

We have a dedicated construction team who’ve been with us from the beginning and our team of international award winning designers offering their expertise to every project, all bound together with solid British engineering at the core of our buildings. With eco conscious design and specification decisions at the heart of our buildings and a skilled team of craftsmen we can ensure that every detail is taken care of.
